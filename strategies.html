Trading Strategies

VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)

VWAP gives a true average price based on volume. Traders buy when price crosses above VWAP, sell below it.

Momentum Trading

Catch stocks moving fast, usually due to news. Look for strong volume + continuation.

Breakout Trading

Buy when a stock breaks past resistance with volume. Ride momentum until volume fades.

Reversals

Buy oversold bounces (RSI < 30), short overbought pullbacks (RSI > 70). Confirm with VWAP + volume.
